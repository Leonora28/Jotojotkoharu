Bae

Formed by three people studying at the same private international university. Believes in living life by being true to themselves without being bound by practicality and rigid concepts. The three of them share Hajun’s high class flat. Takes pride in making use of Japanese, English, and Korean in genreless, trendy HIPHOP.
